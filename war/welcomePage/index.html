ElectoLab

An open platform for electoral simulations.

Features

Research Groups

This is a website made for politics researchers and journalist. But anyone can use it!

If you normally work together, investigate together, make things together.

Simulations

We provide you a great tool to make your research easier. Do you wonder who is going to win next elections but only have a few data polls?

Put your data down, and stop speculating, we give you quick statistical results you can rely on.

Communication

You are doing great work. You don't need e-mail, or WhatsApp. Post in your dashboard and your mates can see your results.

Dashboards provide a great enviroment to keep the track on your simulation projects.

Post your results

Do you want to post your results in your social networks? Do you want to use it for your blog or your news paper?

You can embed your result in any webpage as we provide you a public link if you need it.
